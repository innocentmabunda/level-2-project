“There is no great joy than saving a soul.”











Your blood saves lives. Under 1% of South Africans are dynamic blood benefactors. A unit of blood just a short time after gift and, therefore, it is significant for blood benefactors to give routinely. Contributors can give blood as frequently as like clockwork. Each unit of blood can save at least three lives as blood is isolated into red platelets, plasma and platelets.
